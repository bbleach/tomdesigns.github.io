Payment

As I am under the legal working age, I'm not offering paid services at this time.
As of now I will offer free work to charitable and non-profit organisations.


I am flexible regarding payment - for every unique project I will design a payment method that suits both parties. This may consist of upfront payment, installments or a deposit-and-final-payment system.


All payments will be handles through a secure protocol that protects both parties. Usually, this will be paypal - however cash-in-hand is also possible if we have a face-to-face consultation.


I will provide a full receipt with all invoices however information regarding payment may be requested at any time.
